# Echo

## Content
 * **-e** - enable interpretation of backslash escapes
 * **-E** - opposite of -e
 
 ```console
 # echo <?options> <text | variables>
 foo@bar:~$ echo -e "I care with backslash \n and you?"
 ```
 
## Examples
```console

foo@bar:~$ echo -e "I care with backslash \n and you?"
```
